What to Expect

History & Planning

We take time to understand your background and current concerns and identify the key experiences to focus on during therapy.

Preparation

You’ll be guided through what to expect from EMDR and introduced to simple techniques to help you feel calm, steady and in control throughout the process.

Assessment

Together we will identify a specific upsetting or distressing memory to work on along with the thoughts, feelings and physical sensations connected to it.

Desensitisation

You will gently focus on the memory while engaging in bilateral stimulation such as guided eye movements, tapping, or sounds. Over time this helps reduce the intensity and emotional impact of the memory.

Installation

A more positive and supportive belief is strengthened in its place as the distress linked to the memory decreases.

Body Scan

You’ll be invited to notice any remaining tension or discomfort in the body so allowing anything unresolved to be processed further.

Closure

Each session ends with a focus on feeling settled, safe and grounded before you leave.

Re-evaluation

At the start of later sessions we review progress and identify any new areas to work on.
