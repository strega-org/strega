EMDR

When the past shows up you don't have to face it alone.

EMDR can help the brain process difficult memories so that they feel less overwhelming.

EMDR may help if you

  • Relive painful or intrusive memories, feel on edge or overwhelmed
  • Live with constant worry or low mood or have difficulty managing emotions
  • Have difficulty coping with distress experience low self esteem.

If this feels familiar you are welcome to get in touch for a free consultation

About EMDR at Strega Health

EMDR is an evidence based therapy that help with problems that feel stuck. Strega Health offers a trauma informed EMDR therapy in a calm, supportive setting.
